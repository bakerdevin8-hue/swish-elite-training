Programs

Choose the work that matches your season.

01

Vertimax Power Work

Resistance-band workouts on the Vertimax system to build explosive strength, body control, and a stronger vertical jump.

  • Jump mechanics and landing control
  • Band-resisted first-step work
  • Lower-body power circuits
02

Grind Machine Shooting

The Grind Machine rebounds for players so they can get up more quality shots, build rhythm, and keep reps moving under fatigue.

  • High-rep catch-and-shoot work
  • Game-speed spot shooting
  • Faster reps without chasing rebounds
03

Private Skill Build

Individual sessions for handle, shooting mechanics, finishing package, footwork, and position-specific reads.

  • Weekly take-home reps
04

Small Group Lab

Competitive workouts with tight coach feedback, live constraints, and game-speed pressure.

  • 2-4 athletes per group
  • Decision drills and contests
05

In-Season Tune-Up

Low-volume, high-quality sessions that keep touch, rhythm, and confidence sharp between games.

  • Shot maintenance
  • Role-specific game prep
